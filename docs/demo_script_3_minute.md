# Three-Minute Demo Script

## 0:00-0:15 Opening

Say:

Analytics Workstation is an evidence-governed AI investigation platform. It does not start by producing a dashboard or a report. It starts with uncertainty and turns that uncertainty into a traceable recommendation.

Open Analytics Workstation and navigate to `More -> Build Week Demo`.

## 0:15-0:35 Objective and Preflight

Say:

This demo uses a deterministic synthetic enrollment dataset. The ground truth is hidden from the demo path. Before the workstation acts, it checks that the provider, dataset, analytical services, report browser, replay, and runtime contracts are ready.

Click `Run Preflight`.

## 0:35-1:05 Launch Investigation

Say:

The workstation launches a governed investigation. It records the objective, the initial observation, and the uncertainty before it recommends anything.

Click `Launch Demo`.

## 1:05-1:35 Competing Explanations

Say:

Instead of jumping to one answer, the system keeps competing explanations visible: Search saturation, creative fatigue, operational delay, competitor pressure, audience mix, and regional effects.

Point to the inquiry record.

## 1:35-2:05 Evidence and Belief Revision

Say:

Deterministic services collect evidence. EDA checks the data, regression provides a first driver story, and SHAP is governed behind an approval gate. As the evidence accumulates, the belief changes.

Emphasize:

The AI changed its mind because the evidence changed.

## 2:05-2:25 Recommendation Evolution

Say:

The recommendation evolves from an incomplete first explanation into a more specific action path: improve operational throughput, refresh aging creative, tune saturated Search spend instead of cutting it blindly, and account for competitor pressure separately.

## 2:25-2:50 Claim Verification

Click `Why should I believe this?`.

Say:

The workstation can answer why the recommendation deserves attention. It shows the initial belief, the evidence discovered, the belief revisions, diagnostics, methodology, limitations, and remaining uncertainty.

## 2:50-3:15 Integrity Review

Say:

The final step is skeptical review. The system searches for reasons its own recommendation could be wrong: alternative explanations, contradictory evidence, assumptions, evidence gaps, sensitivity, generalizability, and decision robustness.

Point to Decision Readiness.

## 3:15-3:30 Close

Say:

This is the core idea: Analytics Workstation does not just generate answers. It conducts an investigation, preserves the reasoning, verifies the claims, and critiques its own recommendation before asking anyone to trust it.
